The jQuery Foundation makes extensive use of Internet Relay Chat for user support and realtime project communication. Getting connected is a great way to get help…or get involved!

Channels

All of our channels are hosted on Freenode. If you've never connected to IRC before, or you're just a bit rusty, take a look at our IRC Help page to get up to speed.

#jquery
Support chat for users of jQuery, jQuery UI, and jQuery Mobile. Whether you need help, or you want to help others, or both, this is the place to be.
#jquery-dev
jQuery Core development chat
#jqueryui-dev
jQuery UI development chat
#jquerymobile-dev
jQuery Mobile development chat
#jquery-content
Discussion of work on jQuery Foundation websites, including content, documentation, and design
#jquery-infrastructure
Discussion of work on jQuery Foundation servers, DNS, deployments, and other related infrastructure
#jquery-meeting
Each development team has weekly public meetings at a scheduled time in this channel. The channel is typically +m (no on can talk) unless there is a meeting taking place.

Logging

Each of our channels is publicly logged to this site. Use the menu on the left to browse each channel's logs.
